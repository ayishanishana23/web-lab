COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology (ICET),Mulavoor P.O., Muvattupuzha is a co-educational minority institution approved by AICTE,New Delhi and affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Kerala Technological University (KTU).The college is established in the year 2002. Within 21 years since its inception ICET has made many creditable achievements.

ICET was affiliated to Mahatma Gandhi University , Kerala from the academic year 2002-03 to 2017-18. Last batch of admission to the Mahatma Gandhi University ,Kerala was 2014-18.As per the government of Kerala ACT No. 17 of 2015 , a new technical university named APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) was started in the academic year 2015-16 by merging all the engineering colleges affiliated to various universities in Kerala and first batch of students were admitted to all engineering colleges under this university.Simultaneously along with KTU 2015-16 batch 2012-13,2013-14,2014-15 batches of Mahatma Gandhi University students were also studied till the completion of their courses.
